## PRODUCTION TIPS

### Equipment Needed:
- **Smartphone** with good camera (iPhone/Android)
- **Tripod** or phone stabilizer
- **Ring light** for good lighting
- **Editing app** (CapCut, InShot, Adobe Rush)

### Filming Tips:
1. **Vertical format** (9:16 ratio) for TikTok/Reels
2. **Good lighting** - natural light or ring light
3. **Stable shots** - use tripod
4. **Clear text** - large, readable fonts
5. **High contrast** - text should pop

### Editing Tips:
1. **Fast cuts** - keep it dynamic
2. **Trending music** - use popular sounds
3. **Text animations** - make text engaging
4. **Transitions** - smooth between scenes
5. **Captions** - always add for accessibility

### Text Overlay Best Practices:
- **Font:** Bold, sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica, Impact)
- **Size:** Large enough to read on mobile
- **Color:** High contrast (white text on dark background or vice versa)
- **Position:** Center or bottom third
- **Duration:** 2-3 seconds per text card minimum
