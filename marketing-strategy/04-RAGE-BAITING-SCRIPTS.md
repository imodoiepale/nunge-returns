# RAGE BAITING SCRIPTS - NUNGE RETURNS

## INTRODUCTION TO RAGE BAITING

Rage baiting is a content strategy that triggers emotional responses (frustration, anger, disbelief) to drive engagement. When done ethically, it highlights real pain points and positions your solution as the relief.

**Key Principles:**
1. Highlight genuine frustrations (don't fabricate)
2. Validate the emotion (empathy first)
3. Offer the solution (your service)
4. Keep it relatable and shareable

## ETHICAL GUIDELINES FOR RAGE BAITING

### DO's:
✅ Highlight real problems people face
✅ Use humor to lighten the frustration
✅ Offer genuine solutions
✅ Be relatable and authentic
✅ Validate people's emotions

### DON'Ts:
❌ Fabricate problems that don't exist
❌ Attack individuals or groups
❌ Spread misinformation
❌ Create unnecessary fear
❌ Be insensitive to real struggles

## PERFORMANCE METRICS

### Success Indicators:
- **Engagement Rate:** >15% (higher than standard content)
- **Share Rate:** >10% (rage content is highly shareable)
- **Comment Rate:** >5% (people love to vent)
- **Conversion Rate:** >20% (urgency drives action)
- **Viral Potential:** 1 in 5 rage posts should hit >100K views

### A/B Testing:
- Test different levels of "rage" intensity
- Monitor sentiment (should be frustrated → relieved, not just angry)
- Track conversion from rage content vs educational content
- Adjust tone based on audience response
