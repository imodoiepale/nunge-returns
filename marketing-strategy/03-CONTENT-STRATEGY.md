## CONTENT MISSION & GOALS

### Mission Statement
Create educational, entertaining, and empowering content that demystifies tax compliance for young Kenyans while building trust and driving conversions.

### Content Goals
1. **Awareness:** Reach 500K young Kenyans across social platforms
2. **Education:** Teach tax compliance basics to 100K users
3. **Engagement:** Achieve 10% average engagement rate
4. **Conversion:** Drive 20% of traffic to paid services
5. **Retention:** Build a community of 50K active followers

**MONDAY - Motivation Monday**

- Example: "This Monday, get your KRA PIN sorted. Your future self will thank you."

- Theme: Educational how-to content
- Content: Step-by-step guides, tutorials
- Example: "How to File Nil Returns in 30 Seconds (Tutorial)"

- Theme: Tax tips and financial literacy
